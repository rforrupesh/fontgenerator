Do you want to translate your plain text into something like Crossed-Out Text?

In general, this is also called Strikethrough. It is very useful for highlighting, marking deletions, or indicating outdated information.

Strikethrough is an effective way to show that certain words are no longer relevant but were previously included. Sometimes, you may want to keep some text in a document but indicate that it is no longer applicable—this is where the Strikethrough effect comes in handy.

How Does It Work?

The Strikethrough Text Generator works on Unicode characters. It is a simple tool that adds a horizontal line through text, making words or letters appear as if they are cut in half. To use our tool, simply type your word, and it will automatically convert it into strikethrough text. It also offers different styles, such as:

  • Double underline
  • Wavy line
  • Dotted line
  • S̷l̷a̷s̷h̷ ̷S̷y̷m̷b̷o̷l̷

A copy-and-paste feature is also included in the tool, which helps you copy converted strikethrough text to your clipboard.

How to Apply Strikethrough Formatting

In this section, you will easily learn how to apply strikethrough formatting on various platforms like Word, Excel, WordPress, and others.

Strikethrough in Word

  1. Select your desired text.
  2. Go to Home > Click on the abc button.

Add Strikethrough in Excel

  1. Select the cell.
  2. Click on Format Cells > Font, and check the Strikethrough box.

Strikethrough Text in WordPress Posts or Pages

  1. Select the desired text.
  2. Click on the Dropdown menu > Select S.

How to Remove Strikethrough Text Effect

If you have strikethrough text and want to convert it back to normal text, our tool can help:

  • Go to the Remove tab.
  • Paste your strikethrough text, and the tool will automatically display the original text.
